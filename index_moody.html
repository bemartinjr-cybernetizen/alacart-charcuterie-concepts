Meet the maker

Meet Rebecca

A La Cart Charcuterie Co. is the work of Rebecca, and she is the heart of it. From her home base in Lawrence, Kansas, she builds nearly every grazing table, board, cup, and box herself, and styles each one on site for weddings and events across the region.

What she pours into the work is the difference: a sense of etiquette, beauty, and timing that clients say they have not experienced even with other charcuterie companies. Guests do not just eat what she creates, they photograph it, talk about it, and ask who made it.

She has grown A La Cart Charcuterie Co. almost entirely by word of mouth, one beautifully built table at a time. For Rebecca, it has never been about volume, it is about making something generous, elegant, and personal for every event she touches.
