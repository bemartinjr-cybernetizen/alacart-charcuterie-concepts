Hi, I'm Rebecca

A La Cart Charcuterie Co. is my Lawrence, Kansas based labor of love. I build every grazing table, board, cup, and box by hand and style it on site, treating presentation as seriously as the ingredients.

What my customers say means everything: they do not just eat the spread, they remember it. That attention to detail is the whole point.
