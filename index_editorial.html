Our story

Hand built, styled on site, made to be remembered

A La Cart Charcuterie Co. is a Lawrence, Kansas based maker of grazing tables, boards, cups, and boxes for weddings, parties, and everything in between. Rebecca is the artist behind every spread.

The reviews say it best. Guests do not just eat what she builds, they photograph it, talk about it, and ask who made it. That attention to detail is the whole point.
